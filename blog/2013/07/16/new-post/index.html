16 July 2013

Yesterday we started Phase 2, and its super fuckin badass. We are making websites with sinatra already, they arent super complex but being able make something sweet looking as well as functional after spending a shit load of time making command line apps, its pretty sweet.

The Dragonflies started yesterday and they seem to be doing quite well. One pair was already doing Battleship which I dont think anyone in THE PLAGUE* had done on the first day.
