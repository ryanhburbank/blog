First Day at DevBootcamp

24 June 2013

So today was my first day at DevBootcamp Chicago. I am not sure how to describe it, so Ill just use a single word: Badass. Everyone is super nice and so pumped to be here, its nuts.

This morning our group was welcomed by a man with a pretty cool mustache in short jean shorts and a guy in strobing sun glasses. It threw me off a bit.

Then when we walked into the front door, we walked through a tunnel of screaming people, one of the more terrifying moments of my life.

Most of the morning consisted of talks about what we will be doing and different stuff while we are here.

We did get to start a bit of coding today, which was fun, we got to do pair programming, which I am not so sure on, I will make a seperate post including my thoughts and whatnot on that as a beginner after I send this one out.
