-3 Days

21 June 2013

Only 3 more days until my start at DBC. Im nervous, but not like a nervous that I may do bad, more like a excited nervous, because I am only 3 days away from being dunked into a tank of knowledge.

I’m so glad I was able to find a place like DevBootcamp, last year after I finished high school I was looking all over for web development programs and more traditional colleges. I visited RMCAD in Denver, CO and that was a really cool school but it was more art than development, which if I were to go somewhere for traditional design and art that is where I would go, it was amazing. But I want to code. I also looked at Computer Science Degrees and wasnt really sold on them.

Then about 3 months ago I was reading CPU Magazine and in the very back there was a short article about DevBootcamp, I barely started reading the article and instantly knew I wanted to go there, and now here I am.

Im going to try to start a pattern of when I blog so I force myself to do it everyday so I can write things about what I am learning and help myself remember and also, mark my progress, so in the future I can look back and see how I progressed.
