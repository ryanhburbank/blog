Ruby Hack Night Last Night, DBC Speaker Tonight

Jun 13th, 2013

Last night I went to Ruby Hack Night, which was hosted by Enova, with Micah and Priya from DBC, it was pretty awesome. I got to try out Pair Programming, which I still dont fully get but it was pretty cool. We built a JSON oranizer, my partner knew much more than me and proposed we do it with RSPEC which turned out pretty cool. I had only used RSPEC one other time and it was during the Hartl tutorial, but I really didnt understand it too well. I think I get it a little more now.

Tonight I am going to DBC for the Thursday Speaker Night, the speaker is Eric Lunt who is the CTO of BrightTag, which Im not entirely sure what it does but Im sure Ill understand more after tonight.
