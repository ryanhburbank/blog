~4 Days Out

Jun 20th, 2013

My DevBootcamp experience starts in just over 4 days, and I am getting pretty nervous. I feel like I am ready but I really dont know. I am going to the DBC office tonight for speaker night, which should be awesome as usual. Ive been reading a book called Creating Command Line Applications in Ruby ( I think thats right ) which has been pretty cool, Im not sure how useful it will be but Ive been just reading it along Well Grounded Rubyist which I am sure will be useful because Ive heard people mention how good it is.

Ive also been struggling with internet in the apartment, I bought a ClearWire hotspot last week when I moved here and at some times its awesome but usually its not, which is my biggest problem with it, if it was always shitty that would be fine I guess because I just need it for downloading files and reading but sometimes its really good. When its really good I get the smart idea to stream some music or a movie while I am working then it takes a shit again and it just pisses me off. I decided to just tether off my phone, its not real fast but its consistant. So last night I call up ClearWire to cancel and some “internet expert” attempts to fix it, then blames the inconsistant internet on me not shutting down the hotspot. How am I supposed to use the internet if its shut down? The greatest thing during the whole conversation is when I learned that I’ve used over 40GB of data in just over a week, I was quite impressed seeing how I hadnt downloaded anything significant, just a few ebooks. Pretty nuts.
