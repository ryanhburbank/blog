2 Days

22 June 2013

Well we are only 2 days away from starting DevBootcamp. I am feeling much better about where I am, and I think I am pretty well prepared, only time will tell though.

Last night, Brett (who is my roomate and also a Grasshopper) and I were working on a One Die Game from rubyquiz, we ended up using Nitrous.IO to collaborate over a couple text files and we made a lot of progress last night. We still need to implement computer logic so you can play against AI. We also took it a step further and starting making it so you could specify who many players you wanted and how many of them would be humans, so you could play against 20+ computers (probably wouldnt be very fun) or play vs a friend or something.
