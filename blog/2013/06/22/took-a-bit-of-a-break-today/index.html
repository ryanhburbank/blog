Took a bit of a break today

22 June 2013

This morning my mom was driving through Chicago so I went and had breakfast with her, and then coded a bit, then Brett and I decided to go get groceries and we ended up exploring Chicago a bit, we went to the top of the John Hancock building and took some cool pictures, I played around with Photosphere and then we went and looked at some beautiful cars at a, dare I say, Super / Hyper Car dealership. Cars are so beautiful, even the shitty ones still have a little something that makes them nice. Not sure if I should admit I feel that way though, some cars are so ugly.

Tonight we are working on making that One – Dice – Roll game multiplayer in the sense that you could controll it from 2 or more different computers, we started with sockets but we cant get it to communicate properly.

I suggested we set up something like port forwarding so we could use external IP’s to connect to each other, and I think that would work, but we arent getting it.

So now were kinda reverse engineering multiplayer games built in Ruby to figure out what makes a server and client app communicate.

Also just over 1 day until DevBootcamp starts.

We so excited and it ain’t even Friday.
